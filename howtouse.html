How to use

Just click on the streets you like and wait for the heatmap pattern to appear. Streets areas that are more vibrant and colorful will be represented by red regions.
The heatmap will update itself, but you can make the process faster by clicking on multiple street points. Zoom in and out to change the detail resolution of the visualization.
