Research

Vivid Streets extracts two types of information in order to measure vibrancy in streets. The first type is the patterns of contrast generated by the structures present in the scene. The second type is the color space spanned by the streetscape.
The figure below demonstrates the image processing flow executed by the app. After extracting contrast and color information, Vivid Streets uses statistical methods to combine their data and finally generate ratings for each street.
